Venice Interior Design Services

Transform your Venice home with JAC Interiors' expert interior design services. Creating beautiful spaces that reflect the unique character of Venice.

Venice interior design open concept bungalow with natural wood accents

When you think of Venice, CA, there's a certain soul to it, that unmistakable fusion of creativity, boho spirit, and California Coastal cool. At JAC Interiors, we don’t just design for Venice, we design with it. From breezy walk streets to architectural modern homes along Riviera Ave and timeless craftsman bungalows on Nowita Place, we understand how light, air, color, and setting converge in this vibrant beachside community.

Our design approach is deeply rooted in the vibe of the Westside. Light-filled spaces, surf-ready storage, and furnishings that nod to your love of travel or tech, our interior designers in Venice specialize in creating homes that feel as individualized, effortless, and elevated as the people who live in them.

Whether you’re updating your Abbot Kinney loft with minimalist European flair or bringing new life to a Venice Canals beach cottage, JAC Interiors offers full-service interior design tailored to the aesthetic and energy of where you live and how you live.

Why Choose JAC Interiors in Venice?

1. We Think (and Live) Locally

Our designers spend their mornings walking the dog along Rose Ave and their weekends thumbing through fabric swatches at HD Buttercup. We’re locals who know how the Westside lives day-to-day—and we design for it with intention.

2. Designs Aligned with Beach Living

Salt air, soft light, and the ever-changing hues of the ocean mean your Venice interiors should flow with the climate. We select finishes and furnishings meant to withstand humidity, UV exposure, and sandy bare feet.

3. Coastal Cool + Curated Luxury

We blend the relaxed elegance of West Coast casual with layers of refined design—think organic textures, custom millwork, artisan lighting, and that pop of bold modern art from Bergamot Station we know will light up your space.

4. We Love the Process as Much as the Result

Our collaborative approach places you at the center. We provide design clarity, communication, and calendar-based execution, so even Venice’s most complex renovation projects stay on time and aligned with your beachy-luxe vision.

Local Resources & Collaborators

When sourcing for projects in Venice, we turn first to hometown heroes. Our go-to’s? Garde’s Venice boutique for world-class decorative objects and clean, artful lines. If it’s a kitchen refresh, we’ll coordinate with Ferguson Bath, Kitchen & Lighting Gallery in Santa Monica for quality plumbing fixtures that balance the laid-back Venice look with longevity. For art, we love working with Venice-based installation artist Tahiti Pehrson for statement paper cut sculptures that cast dreamy, shifting shadows across your space.

And when lighting requires that perfect blend of California modern elegance? We always browse Lightopia nearby in Manhattan Beach, they get the beach-meets-design brief like no one else.

Featured Project Highlights

Venice Boho House

This custom-built home sits atop a hill in Venice, offering ocean views and breezes throughout.We collaborated with the developer and homeowner to create a modern farmhouse with a boho-chic style that tells the unique story of the family living there.The design features numerous bespoke touches, including a hand-selected matte fireplace slab and one-off lighting pieces that bring a beachy Southern California vibe to life.The property maximizes every inch of space, with a pool, pool house, outdoor firepit, and an outdoor kitchen for entertainment, making it the perfect model for Venice interior design.

Venice Beach House

This Venice Beach townhouse serves as a study in color play for New York transplants working in the entertainment industry.The young professional couple wanted a "grown up" look that wasn't traditional beachy California coastal design.We adapted their favorite color palettes to create a house as unique as their personalities, featuring retro Arte wallpaper and a double-height art gallery wall curated from their treasured art collection.The custom floating media console doubles as a wine bar and measures almost 20 feet long. This project highlights our ability to create sophisticated, personal, and visually compelling spaces for Venice's creative clientele.
