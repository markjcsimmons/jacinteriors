Why Choose JAC Interiors in Venice?

Our designers spend their mornings walking the dog along Rose Ave and their weekends thumbing through fabric swatches at HD Buttercup. We’re locals who know how the Westside lives day-to-day—and we design for it with intention.

2. Designs Aligned with Beach Living

Salt air, soft light, and the ever-changing hues of the ocean mean your Venice interiors should flow with the climate. We select finishes and furnishings meant to withstand humidity, UV exposure, and sandy bare feet.

Local Resources & Collaborators

When sourcing for projects in Venice, we turn first to hometown heroes. Our go-to’s? Garde’s Venice boutique for world-class decorative objects and clean, artful lines. If it’s a kitchen refresh, we’ll coordinate with Ferguson Bath, Kitchen & Lighting Gallery in Santa Monica for quality plumbing fixtures that balance the laid-back Venice look with longevity. For art, we love working with Venice-based installation artist Tahiti Pehrson for statement paper cut sculptures that cast dreamy, shifting shadows across your space.

And when lighting requires that perfect blend of California modern elegance? We always browse Lightopia nearby in Manhattan Beach, they get the beach-meets-design brief like no one else.
