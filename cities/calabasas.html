Calabasas Interior Designer

Transform your Calabasas home with JAC Interiors' expert interior design services. Creating beautiful spaces that reflect the unique character of Calabasas.

Calabasas is often associated with being the home of the Kardashians, but the LA burb is much more than a haven for Hollywood’s elite. Spend the day outdoors hiking, biking, and picnicking at Malibu Creek State Park or Calabasas Creek Park: you are literally minutes from both the ocean and the mountains.

Boasting loads of trendy restaurants galore, one of Calabasas most famous haunt is Sugarfish by Sushi Nozawa. A top sushi hang of choice in Calabasas and it’s well worth the wait. Just make sure you show up hungry!

And if by chance you are looking for an authentic sanctuary crafted by artisans from India, look no further! The Malibu Hindu Temple looks like a movie set but actually offers a little bit of serenity in paradise. You can make use of the temple’s several public spaces like a its meditation areas and the peaceful manicured greens where you can enjoy a picnic and a roll in the grass. There are numerous celebrations, ceremonies, and gatherings at the temple, including cultural programs on the regular, so check it out!
