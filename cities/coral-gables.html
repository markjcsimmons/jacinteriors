The Vibe

Living in Coral Gables

Nestled among swaying palms, Mediterranean Revival architecture, and some of South Florida’s most charming avenues, Coral Gables is where laid-back luxury meets timeless distinction. At JAC Interiors, we’re thrilled to bring our deeply tailored, full-service luxury interior design capabilities to this vibrant, historic enclave.

Whether you live near the lush estates along Granada Boulevard, in a sun-drenched condo over on Ponce de Leon, or in a secluded retreat on Old Cutler Road, our design team brings a fresh, elevated point of view to Coral Gables living. With years of experience designing homes in California, Colorado, and now Florida, we deeply understand how climate, light, and lifestyle shape great design. And in Coral Gables, that means embracing tropical light, cross-ventilation, hurricane-resilient materials, and coastal textures—without ever sacrificing sophistication or warmth.

With a design process rooted in collaborative trust and an eye for layered details, JAC Interiors is here to design your Coral Gables home as though it were our own.

Approach

Why JAC Interiors

We understand the demands of South Florida’s climate—humidity, salt air, hurricane season. That’s why we incorporate storm-rated windows, moisture-resistant finishes, and durable outdoor textiles into every design. It’s beauty built to last.

Coral Gables is rich with Mediterranean, Art Deco, and Mission-style homes. We know how to preserve those traditional elements while seamlessly layering in clean-lined modern pieces, artisanal accents, and coastal California ease.

As a boutique studio, we bring a hands-on, personalized approach to every Coral Gables project. Led by founding partners Andrea Putman and Timothy Mersman—both Otis grads—we combine a strong design vision with rigorous project management and technical expertise.

From sourcing hurricane-rated outdoor furnishings at Clima Outdoor in Coral Gables to selecting sleek plumbing fixtures at Ferguson on Bird Road, we work with the local showrooms that deliver both quality and style.

Our clients call us warm, enthusiastic, discerning. And it's true: every detail of your home—from crown moldings to custom closets—is approached with the same care we give our own spaces.
