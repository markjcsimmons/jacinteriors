The Vibe

Living in Doral

At JAC Interiors, we're more than just interior designers—we’re your creative collaborators, coast-to-coast. And now, with our continued expansion into Florida’s Gold Coast, we’ve landed right here in Doral. Nestled just west of Miami, Doral's sunny, vibrant energy and mix of Mediterranean modern homes, sleek new builds, and townhome communities create the perfect canvas for statement-making interiors. We know this terrain well—what it means to balance bold aesthetics with daily function in a city that moves fast and basks in subtropical warmth. From hurricane-ready materials to interiors that embrace that incredible South Florida light, our Doral interior design services are curated to your lifestyle, neighborhood, and home type.

Whether you're cultivating a breezy, indoor-outdoor vibe or elevating a high-rise apartment with layered sophistication, JAC Interiors is your go-to Doral interior designer for signature luxury with a playful twist.

Approach

Why JAC Interiors

With established roots in California and Colorado, and now designing across Florida, we’re no strangers to tailoring design to regional needs. From managing humidity and salt corrosion to optimizing light filtering, our experience designing for climate-conscious living shines here.

In Doral, durability is key—but it should never come at the expense of beauty. We source materials and vendors that stand up to South Florida's high humidity while maintaining a luxe aesthetic.

From large-format porcelain for your lanai floor to custom retractable shade solutions, our interiors flow seamlessly from indoor family rooms to sun-drenched patios and Florida rooms.

Just like our Crafty Hustle ethos promises, we’re fastidious when it comes to managing timelines, budgets, and vendor relations. We sweat the details, so you don’t have to.

Our process is geared around discovery and transformation. We don’t shoehorn styles. Whether you're drawn to Miami Modern with gloss-lacquered cabinetry or leaning toward Santa Monica-meets-South Florida textures like organic linens and driftwood tones—we’ll make it beautifully yours.
