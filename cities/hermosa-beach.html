Why Choose JAC Interiors in Hermosa Beach?

We’re not just a luxury interior design studio—we’re part of this community. From early morning stops at Becker’s Bakery to sunset walks past Hermosa Pier, we understand what makes this city tick, and we design accordingly.

Here’s why our clients trust us to elevate their Hermosa Beach homes:

1. Local Understanding = Smarter Design

The fog rolls in fast here. Salt air corrodes cheaper metals. We account for coastal conditions from materials to maintenance—like opting for rust-proof hardware and easy-breezy fabrics that stand up to sand and sun.

2. Inside Access to Trusted Vendors

We collaborate with local heavy-hitters like Arteriors on Melrose for statement lighting, and work closely with Hermosa Tile + Stone for custom surf-inspired backsplashes that blend with your aesthetic—not battle it.

3. Boutique Feel, Big Impact

Our team is small but mighty. We treat every home like it’s our own, which means you get hands-on service directly from partners Andrea Putman andTimothy Mersman—who both bring fashion-meets-function sensibility rooted in Otis School of Arts and Sciences training.

4. Seamlessly Project Managed

From Hermosa Avenue permits to coordinating contractors up Pier Avenue, our Crafty Hustle means we handle the red tape and stress so you don’t have to.

5. Style with a Soulful Twist

Hermosa vibes aren't sterile. Neither are we. We love infusing texture, warmth, and a hit of unexpected design—like a vintage surfboard as entry decor or Moroccan zellige tiles in a powder bath—because following the rules isn’t the only way to design beautifully.

Local Resources & Collaborators

One of the best things about being a Hermosa Beach interior designer is tapping into the South Bay’s overflowing creative resources.

We frequent the South Bay Homebuilding Center in nearby Redondo Beach for unique wood paneling and cabinetry finishes. When we're after vintage or reclaimed items, Coast to Coast Imports on Pacific Coast Highway often has just the thing. For lighting, the Arteriors showroom in the La Cienega Design Quarter is our go-to—it’s worth every drive up the 405 for their seasonal collections and curated designer collabs.

And for those serene coastal palettes? We regularly source custom wall treatments and woven accents from our favorite artisans around Abbot Kinney and the Hermosa Art Walk community.
