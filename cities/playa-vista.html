Living in Playa Vista

Welcome to Playa Vista, home of tech meets texture, where innovation pulses through Runway’s cafes and design-forward living is just a way of life. At JAC Interiors, we’re not just interior designers—we’re your Playa Vista neighbors, perched right alongside you in this vibrant, future-thinking enclave of the Westside. Whether you’re in a modern New Urbanism condo along Bluff Creek Drive or an elegant home in The Collection, our Playa Vista interior design services blend laid-back luxury with personalized artistry.

With the neighborhood’s signature warm coastal light, proximity to the ocean’s breezes, and the sleek, often minimalistic architectural styles, interior design in Playa Vista calls for a nuanced approach. Our goal? To create bold, comfortable, personality-packed spaces that reflect you, while honoring the upscale, modern vibe of local living.

Our Services

As your go-to Playa Vista interior designer, JAC Interiors delivers full-service, end-to-end luxury design including:

  • Full-Service Interior Design (floor plans, 3D rendering, sourcing + install)
  • Room-Specific Design (kitchens, baths, living spaces, bedrooms, home offices)
  • Custom Cabinetry & Millwork (perfect for those Playa Vista built-ins)
  • Lighting Design (dialing up drama or natural ease)
  • Remodel + Design-Build Services
  • Art Curation with a SoCal edge
  • Project Management from start to fabulous finish

Built around collaboration and your personal aesthetic, we approach each home as a canvas. Efficiency, flow, and storytelling matter to us as much as visual texture and dreamy materials.

Why JAC Interiors

We live and breathe the local lifestyle, and design with it in mind:

1. Understanding of Urban + Tech-Influenced Architecture

From The Lofts at Playa Vista to Camden and Skylar homes, we understand the architectural landscape and optimize interiors for scale, light, and connectivity.

2. Tailored Designs for Westside Living

Close to the beach yet nestled in upwardly mobile neighborhoods, your home has to breathe. We layer airy material palettes, clean lines, and natural textures to complement Play Vista’s climate and light.

3. Smart Design for Smart Homes

With many Playa Vista clients hailing from nearby tech and media giants (hey, Google and YouTube!), we integrate design-forward, tech-savvy features that don’t skimp on style.

4. Our Insider Resources, At Your Doorstep

We source furniture, art, and accessories from cult-favorite LA vendors—think curated finds from Big Daddy’s Antiques on Jefferson Blvd, low-profile seating and sculptural lighting from Organic Modernism in Culver City.

5. We Build Relationships, Not Just Rooms

Our Playa Vista clients tend to be well-informed, discerning, and engaged—and that’s our perfect match. We co-create spaces that feel like an extension of you, elevated where it counts, relaxed where it matters.
