Our Signature Interior Design Services in Wynwood & Edgewater

JAC Interiors is your go-to for interior design services in Miami's most expressive neighborhoods. Here’s what we bring to the table:

- Room-Specific Design:Tailored design for open-concept kitchens, bold powder rooms, or serene primary suites.

Why Choose JAC for Interior Design in Wynwood / Edgewater?

In Wynwood, we pull cues from local muralists, bringing movement and vibrancy into otherwise minimalist interiors. We know you love bold art, so we lean into expressive palettes, high-contrast textures, and gallery-style lighting to make your space feel like its own exhibit. Meanwhile in Edgewater, the light reflecting off Biscayne Bay is dreamy—so we lean into finishes that play off of that, from high-gloss terrazzo to tinted glass accents that shift as the day progresses.

South Florida’s climate is no joke. We don't just choose beautiful finishes—we choose materials made for Miami. Think moisture-resistant custom wood veneers, mold-resistant paints, and hurricane-impact-rated windows with elegant framing. Designing for durability and drama is what we do.

We shop Wynwood Walls and Art Basel pop-ups, but also import one-of-a-kind European finishes when your home calls for it. One favorite local partner is Díaz Display on NE 2nd Avenue—their ceramic light sculptures are unbeatable for textural ambiance.

Local Collaborators & Showroom Musts
