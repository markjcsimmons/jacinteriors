Living in Valley Village

Tucked between Studio City and North Hollywood, Valley Village blends suburban charm with real-deal Los Angeles creative energy. Here, charming 1930s Spanish homes sit alongside newer modern builds. It’s a neighborhood where design has depth and personality—and that’s exactly why JAC Interiors loves working here.

We bring our full-service luxury interior design offerings to Valley Village with authenticity, collaboration, and a little edge. California light defines our aesthetic choices, and in a community like this—where shade trees, family-friendly streets, and a thriving cultural scene mix—we get to dial in environments that feel both stylish and livable.

Founded by Andrea Putman and Timothy Mersman, and powered by our high-touch team (all trained at the Otis School of Arts and Sciences), JAC Interiors specializes in transforming everyday homes into dream-worthy sanctuaries.

Whether we’re working with one of the stunning mid-century ranches off Laurel Canyon or doing a gut-renovation on a 5-bed Spanish Colonial on Magnolia Boulevard, our process stays the same: collaborative, visionary, and ultra-detailed. We treat every space like it’s our own—with that signature JAC touch.

Why JAC Interiors

From traditional Spanish bungalow details like archways and plaster walls to sleek ADU conversions, we know these Valley Village styles intimately. We're not here to overwrite good bones—we enhance them.

Valley Village is filled with families that want form and function. We blend effortless style with kid-friendly materials, pet-friendly layouts, and reimagined zones that support everything from work-from-home to after-school chaos.

We understand the pace of Los Angeles, and we work closely with seasoned local contractors, cabinet makers, and tradespeople who get things done right—and on your timeline.

From sun-faded wood floors to thermal comfort in the hot months, Valley Village homes present specific design challenges due to our Southern California climate. We integrate material choices that last, reduce energy consumption, and keep your home cool and collected year-round.

We don’t push a design style. Whether your vibe leans minimalist, boho, glam, or transitional Cali-modern, we embrace your perspective and build a space that reflects you—and your neighborhood.
