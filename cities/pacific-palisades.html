Pacific Palisades Interior Design Services

Transform your Pacific Palisades home with JAC Interiors' expert interior design services. Creating beautiful spaces that reflect the unique character of Pacific Palisades.

Nestled between the cliffs of the Pacific and the Santa Monica Mountains, Pacific Palisades offers a rare blend of breezy sophistication, ocean proximity, and architectural prestige. At JAC Interiors, we know this community inside and out, our team has designed everything from mid-century modern hillside remodels in the Marquez Knolls to classic Mediterranean new-builds off Amalfi Drive. As your go-to Pacific Palisades interior designer, we specialize in crafting high-end homes that embrace both function and California-casual elegance.

Living in Pacific Palisades means sun-drenched interiors, pristine ocean views—and yes, the design challenges that come with coastal humidity and shifting light throughout the day. We integrate custom solutions: performance fabrics, bleached oak finishes, and indoor-outdoor flow that’s beautifully resilient. Our designs are rooted in what feels honest and intentional for you, nothing too precious, always personal.

Whether you recently purchased a bluffside property in The Riviera or you’re refreshing your family’s El Medio Cape Cod, we create spaces that are as stylish as they are livable. Think: luxe finishes without the formality, open-plan living with layered soul, and curated design that puts comfort first.

Why Choose JAC in Pacific Palisades?

We live and breathe design, but we also speak fluent Westside. Our work is driven by experience, local insight, and a deep respect for the way Pacific Palisades families want to live.

1. We Know the Light & Landscape

Palisades homes soak in that signature coastal glow, but it can wreak havoc on the wrong finishes. We select materials that amplify natural light without fading or wear, like matte oak floors and warm stone textures that stand up to the sun.

2. Relaxed, Refined Aesthetic

Our signature “California eclectic” style was practically made for this neighborhood: breezy but bold, artful yet functional. We use organic elements, meaningful objects, and layered neutrals with a modern twist.

3. Deep Vendor Relationships

From sourcing terrazzo slabs at Tashman Home Center on Santa Monica Blvd to scoring one-of-a-kind ceramics from Venice-based Commune, our local network gives us an edge when it comes to finding unique finishes and furnishings.

4. Experience with Iconic Architecture

We’ve renovated everything from 1940s beach bungalows near Temescal to sleek contemporary homes off Bienveneda Avenue. Our solutions respect the bones of the home while enhancing modern livability.

5. End-to-End Project Management

Because Palisades clients are often balancing work, family, and travel, we handle the heavy lifting—from contractor scheduling to lamp shade selections—so you can walk into your dream space with zero stress.

Local Resources & Collaborators

Design is a team sport, and we’re proud to work with some of the best local resources in the area.

  • Blackman Showroom in Santa Monica– Our go-to for luxe bath and hardware finishes with a wide range of coastal-appropriate styles.
  • Elizabeth Lamont in the Pacific Palisades Village– Their curated pillows, coastal-inspired ceramics, and global décor bring a warm, layered touch our clients love. A great stop for last-minute styling accessories!
  • Egg & Dart on La Cienega– Still worth the drive for high-end fabrics and elevated trim. Great for sourcing custom drapery details when designing formal living rooms or home libraries.
  • Boffi on Beverly– For our modernist Palisades clients, Boffi is essential for streamlined kitchen + bath solutions that have European polish and Californian soul.
