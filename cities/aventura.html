The Vibe

Living in Aventura / Golden Isles

Sophisticated coastal flair, artfully curated living, and vibrant lifestyles—that’s exactly what we design for in Aventura and Golden Isles. At JAC Interiors, we bring West Coast cool and bespoke design expertise to South Florida’s Gold Coast, offering personalized, luxury interior design services that enhance how you live. Whether you’re tucked away in a waterfront high-rise with sunset-facing balconies or nestled within the gated tranquility of Golden Isles, we’re here to transform your space from typical to truly elevated.

Welcome to Florida’s year-round indoor-outdoor paradise, where interiors need to flow as beautifully as Biscayne Bay’s breeze. We don’t just design rooms—we craft environments that honor your lifestyle, with special attention paid to coastal humidity, hurricane-ready materials, and that unbeatable natural light (yes, we see you, golden hour glow). With over a decade of experience in luxury design, and newly expanding our Southern base here, the JAC Interiors team is a natural fit for Aventura and Golden Isles projects both bold and timeless.

Approach

Why JAC Interiors

We’re bringing years of experience from L.A.'s sharp design scene—think Santa Monica meets South Beach. Our interiors fuse laid-back luxury with coastal functionality, using materials that stand up to Florida salt air while still feeling elegant and intentional.

From our L.A. and Colorado offices, we’ve curated homes in Beverly Hills, Venice Beach, Santa Monica, and even luxury resorts like the Yellowstone Club. Now, we’re dialed into what makes Aventura’s luxury towers and Golden Isles estates tick—from smart storage to shaded serenity.

Open concepts for entertaining, breezy layouts that embrace the bay breeze, and hurricane-rated finishes that never sacrifice beauty. Our design approach balances beauty and durability, because here in Aventura, home spaces do double-duty as sanctuaries and social hubs.

We cherry-pick from top-tier vendors locally and nationally, creating curated palettes of teak, rattan, sea glass, quartzite, and saline-proof textiles. Whether we’re upgrading a Collins Avenue condo or a waterfront villa on Holiday Drive, we source from showrooms like LuxaLuxe in the Miami Design District or specialty vendors like Ceramic Matrix for timeless stone finishes.

From HOA restrictions to seasonal install schedules (hello, snowbird timelines!), we handle the nitty gritty, so you can enjoy a smooth, stress-free design journey. You’ll feel it from day one: we’re design pros who treat your home like our own.

When we source and shop in the area, we go high-low: luxury resource houses to niche artisan crafts. We love working with specialty vendors like:

LuxaLuxe Home (in the Design District)– excellent contemporary showroom with Italian cabinetry lines and high-gloss pieces perfect for high-rise styling.

Ceramic Matrix Miami –when you want that perfect sea-toned dolomite for your master bath or coral-inspired terrazzo for your entryway, Ceramic Matrix is where we go.

Armazem Design –ideal for clients craving Brazilian wood pieces and globally curated accessories.

From curated lighting to site-specific drapery installation that works with intense South Florida sun, JAC taps Aventura’s best local craft and design minds, pairing them with our coast-to-coast designer network.

Our Florida design studio is tuned into the styles and floor plans specific to these beautiful enclaves:

Golden Isles –Known for its deepwater dock homes and custom waterfront estates, we can help you maximize views and surfaces that stand up to salt, sun, and seagrass paws (yes, we’re dog lovers too).

Turnberry Isle –Sophisticated yet classic, these residences benefit from custom built-ins, layered lighting, and high-impact window treatments that balance golf-view privacy with panoramic shine.

Porto Vita –Design-forward, resort-style living needs resort-style interiors. We bring hospitality inspirations from projects like Toscana Country Club directly to your doorstep.

Williams Island –We love giving these high-rise homes texture, depth, and warmth—our custom cabinetry and closet designs shine in variable lighting here.

Ventura at Harbor Village –Many of these newer builds are perfect for a boutique refresh with accents like custom powder rooms or wardrobe-worthy walkthrough closets.
