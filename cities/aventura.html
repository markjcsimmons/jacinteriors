Why JAC Interiors is the Go-To Aventura Interior Designer

We’re bringing years of experience from L.A.'s sharp design scene—think Santa Monica meets South Beach. Our interiors fuse laid-back luxury with coastal functionality, using materials that stand up to Florida salt air while still feeling elegant and intentional.

From our L.A. and Colorado offices, we’ve curated homes in Beverly Hills, Venice Beach, Santa Monica, and even luxury resorts like the Yellowstone Club. Now, we’re dialed into what makes Aventura’s luxury towers and Golden Isles estates tick—from smart storage to shaded serenity.

Open concepts for entertaining, breezy layouts that embrace the bay breeze, and hurricane-rated finishes that never sacrifice beauty. Our design approach balances beauty and durability, because here in Aventura, home spaces do double-duty as sanctuaries and social hubs.
