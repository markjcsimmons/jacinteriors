The Vibe

Living in Universal City

Welcome to JAC Interiors, your go-to resource for luxury interior design in the heart of Universal City. Tucked between the Hollywood Hills and the glitz of Studio City, Universal City has a personality all its own—equal parts Old Hollywood charisma and contemporary entertainment pulse. And for us at JAC Interiors, that means endless creative opportunities.

Whether you're revamping a contemporary hillside home with dramatic views of the Cahuenga Pass or curating a chic live/work space near the Universal backlot, our approach is always tailored, always elevated, and always fun. With years of experience designing for L.A.'s most dynamic neighborhoods—including classic craftsman homes in Toluca Lake to sleek penthouses above Ventura Blvd—we bring insider knowledge, impeccable taste, and full-throttle project management to every space we transform.

Let’s design something unforgettable together (yes, even if you’re just around the corner from Hogwarts™).

Our Services

As a full-service interior design studio serving Universal City, we offer complete start-to-finish creative services. Here’s what that looks like for our local clients:

  • Full-Service Interior Design:Floor plans, CAD drafting, mood boards, renderings, and design implementation
  • Custom Furniture Design: Bespoke furniture pieces and built-ins perfectly scaled to your home's unique layout
  • Kitchen & Bath Renovation: Luxury finishes and optimized layouts to elevate daily functionality
  • Lighting Design:Ambient, task, and sculptural lighting strategies that bring homes to life—especially as daylight fades beyond the valley

Approach

Why JAC Interiors

As locals, we intuitively understand that Universal City isn’t just a zip code—it’s a lifestyle straddling work, play, and everything in between. Here’s why our design approach aligns so seamlessly with the area:

Whether you’re hosting an industry dinner or streaming an indie film screener, Universal City homes are meant to dazzle. We create spaces that make an entrance—think bold lighting from Arteriors Melrose or statement marble fireplaces sourced from David Homami Meyer’s Pico showroom.

In neighborhoods like the Hollywood Dell or hillside pockets near Barham Blvd, where square footage is often wonky or tight, we specialize in maximizing form and function. Custom built-ins, hidden storage, and multi-purpose layouts turn challenges into design triumphs.

Homes here benefit from that coveted Southern California light—something we love to highlight with smart fabric selections, layered window treatments, and reflective finishes. From modern Spanish to mid-century ranch updates, our palette choices lean into warmth, softness, and dimension.

Many of our Universal City clients juggle high-pressure production roles and off-hours creativity. Our process is streamlined and enjoyable—from our digital mood boards to our trusted contractor network—so you can stay focused on your next big release while we handle the design drama.

We’ve been sourcing for years in and around Universal City, Curating pieces that not only photograph beautifully but feel incredible in person. Some of our favorites:

  • Arteriors Los Angeles (Melrose Ave): sculptural lighting and conversation-starter statement pieces
  • David Homami Meyer on Pico:unmatched for high-end plumbing fixtures and artisanal finishes
  • Garde on Beverly Blvd: for accessories that read as global, not generic—this is where we go when only the most refined objects will do

We’re fortunate to collaborate with these tried-and-true vendors who help us deliver spaces filled with soul and function.
