Why Choose JAC Interiors in Playa Del Rey

1. We’re Your Coastal Neighbors

We don’t just design in Playa—we’re locals who surf Dockweiler, sip cold brews at Tanner’s, and know exactly where the best natural light hits at 4 p.m. Our proximity means access, responsiveness, and a real understanding of the lifestyle here.

2. We Design for How You Live

This isn’t formal, fussy design packaged in gloss. Our signature look blends bold color choices, clean lines, and tactile materials that stand up to beach life—think performance fabrics, organic woods, local ceramics.

3. Trusted Collaborators, Local Know-How

We rely on trusted local partners including Woven Accents for artisanal rugs, Rejuvenation in Culver City for lighting that elevates any airy beach house, and we hit up Harper Flooring on Lincoln for sustainably sourced hardwoods.

4. We Know the Nuances

Playa’s salt air, year-round sunlight, and layered indoor-outdoor flow necessitate a thoughtful approach—finishes that won’t tarnish, smart window treatments for solar gain, colors that evolve with golden hour.

5. Full-Scope Project Management

From your first sketch to the final styled shelfie, we handle it all. Our process makes it easy, collaborative, and—dare we say—fun.

Local Resources & Collaborators

We’re big believers in celebrating LA’s maker culture. Just up the coast in Venice, you’ll find Christopher Farr Cloth for distinctive, globally inspired textiles, and we regularly source ceramics from KleinReid, available at Tortoise General Store on Abbot Kinney. Looking for lighting that stuns? We often spec from Brendan Ravenhill Studio, whose pieces fuse utility and artistry—perfect for beachfront modern homes.

And when it comes to working with top-tier tradespeople, we've built trusted relationships with local contractors who truly understand the challenges of working in older homes—especially those Post-War cottages just east of Pacific.

Featured Project Highlights

Venice Beach House

This Venice Beach townhouse, a study in color play, is a perfect model for Playa Del Rey homes, as it moves beyond traditional coastal clichés.We created a "grown up" look by adapting our client's favorite color palettes to create a house as unique as their personalities.The design features a double-height art gallery wall curated from their treasured art collection and a custom floating media console that doubles as a wine bar.This project shows how JAC Interiors can create a home that is both personal and polished, with seamless indoor-outdoor connections like the rooftop dining area designed for sunset cocktails.

Santa Monica Modern Spanish

Located nearby, the Santa Monica Modern Spanish project demonstrates our expertise in honoring a home's history while infusing it with modern elegance—a valuable approach for homes in and around Playa Del Rey.We modernized the interior while preserving original period elements like the leaded pane window in the front living room and handsome Pewabic tiles around the fireplace.The design focused on bringing the outdoors inside through texture and color, adding artisan touches that feel fresh while nodding to the home's history and location.
