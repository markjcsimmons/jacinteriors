Living in Playa Del Rey

There’s just something about Playa Del Rey. Tucked between Marina del Rey and El Segundo, this quiet coastal gem offers laid-back beach vibes with truly stunning views. At JAC Interiors, we’ve spent years designing for Playa’s mix of historic bungalows, luxe townhomes, and those airy new builds popping up on Convoy or Waterview. Whether you're nestled on the hill with ocean views or tucked into a charming mid-century home near Sunridge, our team brings elevated, practical interior design that captures everything we love about life by the sand.

We get what it means to live here—where sunny, salt-swept mornings light up your interiors in ways you can’t ignore. That means coastal light management, fabrics that breathe, and finishes that can take a little wear from sandy toes and sea-fresh breezes. Our team balances luxe materials with livable design, making sure your Playa del Rey home reflects your lifestyle and the community you’re proud to call home.

Why Choose JAC Interiors for Playa Del Rey

1. We’re Your Coastal Neighbors

We don’t just design in Playa—we’re locals who surf Dockweiler, sip cold brews at Tanner’s, and know exactly where the best natural light hits at 4 p.m. Our proximity means access, responsiveness, and a real understanding of the lifestyle here.

2. We Design for How You Live

This isn’t formal, fussy design packaged in gloss. Our signature look blends bold color choices, clean lines, and tactile materials that stand up to beach life—think performance fabrics, organic woods, local ceramics.

3. Trusted Collaborators, Local Know-How

We rely on trusted local partners including Woven Accents for artisanal rugs, Rejuvenation in Culver City for lighting that elevates any airy beach house, and we hit up Harper Flooring on Lincoln for sustainably sourced hardwoods.

4. We Know the Nuances

Playa’s salt air, year-round sunlight, and layered indoor-outdoor flow necessitate a thoughtful approach—finishes that won’t tarnish, smart window treatments for solar gain, colors that evolve with golden hour.

5. Full-Scope Project Management

From your first sketch to the final styled shelfie, we handle it all. Our process makes it easy, collaborative, and—dare we say—fun.
