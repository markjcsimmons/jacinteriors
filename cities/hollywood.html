Living in Hollywood

AtJAC Interiors, we bring our signature blend of West Coast vibe, high-function design, and luxury lifestyle to the tropical vibrance ofHollywood, Broward. We know the feeling: you want interiors that feel intentional and elevated, not overly styled or impersonal. Spaces that handle Florida weather but still turn heads. That's where we come in.

This isn’t your cookie-cutter decorating service. JAC is afull-service interior design firmwith a stylishly relaxed (but seriously strategic) approach shaped by more than a decade of experience across Los Angeles, Colorado, and now the Gold Coast of Florida. We’re here to help you reimagine life at home by designing layered interiors that reflect your taste and work seamlessly with South Florida’s lifestyle—from sun-soaked family rooms tohurricane-ready finishesthat give you peace of mind during storm season.

Whether your place is perched alongNorth Ocean Drive, nestled inEmerald Hills, or a modern build just west ofYoung Circle, our team understands the nuances of Hollywood’s indoor-outdoor rhythm and how to design for it intelligently—with style, not stress.

Approach

Why JAC Interiors

With decades of design experience from L.A. to Delray Beach, we understand how South Florida’s humidity, coastal breezes, and sunlight demand different materials and configurations. Our team selects finishes that are asresilient as they are stylish.

Hollywood homes vary wildly—from mid-century stunners offJohnson Streetto newer construction nearWest Lake Park. That’s why every JAC home is distinct. We tailor every design plan to complement your home’s bones, your personal aesthetic, and your lifestyle goals.

We don’t just style spaces, we design them from the ground up. Whether it’s a major bathroom remodel nearHollywood Beachor a full-scale built-in bookshelf inLakes of Emerald Hills, we bring your ideas to life while managing every detail.

Our strong network of exceptional vendors means your project benefits frompremium materials, custom furniture, and statement lighting that’s not found in retail showrooms. Bonus: we handle all the scheduling, ordering, and logistics.

You’ll find us somewhere between creative visionary and logistical powerhouse. We’re here to make the design process (dare we say) enjoyable. Our clients feel relaxed and empowered by our process—we treat every project like it’s our own home.

We’re often sourcing one-of-a-kind marble slabs and exotic woods through MOD Construction Supply in Fort Lauderdale, or working hand-in-glove with the expert cabinetry team atLuxe Kitchen & Bathin Dania Beach. For contemporary lighting installations, Arteriors andLumens’South Florida divisions deliver sophistication and function in equal parts.

Our team is also known to make a pit stop atDeco Domicileoff Federal Highway—because their curated collection of mid-century and Brazilian modern furniture is basically a playground for design lovers. And foroutdoor spaces? We source luxury, coastal-rated materials perfect for balconies, pool decks, and transitional patios from Tropical Hardwoods in Miami.
