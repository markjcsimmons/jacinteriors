North Hollywood as We Know and Love It

North Hollywood isn’t just “up the 170”—it’s an immersive cultural mash-up of creatives, historic architecture, and mid-century palm-lined streets. We’ve watched NoHo transform into a dynamic hub for artists, young professionals, and design-forward homeowners looking to translate their vibe into their living space. Between the art-deco bones of the El Portal Theatre and the sleek new builds cropping up around the NoHo Arts District, we see design opportunity at every corner. At JAC Interiors, we know how to make that vision real—and totally you.

Founded in 2012, JAC Interiors is a full-service interior design studio with deep roots in Los Angeles, and we’ve been lucky enough to work with clients all through the Valley, including here in North Hollywood. From cozy bungalows in Valley Village to modern remodels off Magnolia Blvd., we tailor every detail to the lifestyle, flow, and creative spirit of the people who live there.

Why Choose JAC Interiors in North Hollywood?

A Local’s Take on Design That Speaks to the Neighborhood

From the vintage feel around Lankershim Blvd. to the contemporary lofts near the Red Line station, we love mixing industrial textures with saturated color statements—North Hollywood really calls for it.

Adding function to charming older homes on Morrison Street, Radford Avenue, or those mid-century flats in Toluca Terrace? That’s our jam. We know how to respect the character while enhancing your lifestyle.

The soft, warm light in the Valley hits differently, especially in the late afternoon. We design with that quality in mind—selecting materials and hues that translate beautifully in every hour of the day.

We’ve cultivated strong relationships with trusted vendors like CCF Interiors for custom upholstery and Newport Brass & Lee Valley for hardware and faucets—let’s just say they know us by name (and they should!).

Working on your home should feel exciting—not overwhelming. Our process is collaborative, playful, and always driven by a clear design plan crafted just for you.

Local Resources & Collaborators You’ll Love

When sourcing for projects in North Hollywood, we often make design pilgrimages to:

  • Kreiss on Robertson– An iconic showroom that gives us luxe, layered living room vibes, ideal for open-space plans common around Toluca Woods.
  • Modern Lighting over on La Brea– think sculptural pendants and moody sconces to transform home entries or dining areas.
  • The Valley’s own H.D. Buttercup Studioin Culver City (yes, worth the short drive) is our go-to for modern vintage furniture and those one-of-a-kind wood pieces perfect for adding warmth to your Valley digs.
  • David Homami Meyer on Picois our go-to plumbing whisperer—because the right bath hardware changes everything.
