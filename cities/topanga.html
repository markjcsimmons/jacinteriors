Living in Topanga

Nestled in one of LA’s most storied enclaves, Topanga is where the line between nature and design gets downright poetic. At JAC Interiors, we get it. We don’t just design for Topanga—we design from within it. Our team thrives on that unfiltered canyon light, the scent of eucalyptus drifting through open windows, and the texture of reclaimed wood warmed by the California sun. With over a decade designing across Los Angeles and boutique markets like Topanga, we've honed a design ethos that’s part elevated bohemian, part contemporary chic—with just a touch of refined edge.

As Topanga interior designers, we bring a curated, full-service experience that anchors your home's soul while adapting effortlessly to the lifestyle-rich, spiritual, and creative heartbeat of this unique place.

Whether you're updating a hillside retreat off Old Topanga Canyon Road or dialing in your dream bathroom along Fernwood Pacific Drive, we bring seasoned expertise and authentic local insight to every corner of your transformation.

Our Services

JAC Interiors specializes in full-service interior design that honors your style while layering in the unexpected. We offer:

  • Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling— custom cabinetry, lighting layout, and spa-worthy finishes
  • Whole-Home Renovations & Styling— from 3D renderings to final accessory placement
  • Custom Furniture & Built-Ins— artisan-crafted pieces designed for your dimensions and lifestyle
  • Architectural Coordination & Design-Build— collaboration with trusted contractors and architects
  • Art Curation & Lighting Design— to transform your space into a personalized gallery
  • Outdoor Living Spaces— al fresco-ready patios and canyon-view decks
  • Rebuild LA Program— ideal for Topanga residents rebuilding after wildfire losses

We treat each project like we’re designing our own home—because we know that great design isn’t just beautiful, it works.

Why JAC Interiors

Here’s what makes us local favorites—besides our taste in artisan ceramics and custom plaster walls:

Topanga has its microclimates and quirks—sun-flooded rooms by day, cool, misty evenings by night. We tailor solutions for sustainability, temperature variety, and materials that don’t just survive Topanga... they thrive in it.

Your space should feel soulful, not staged. We layer modern SoCal sophistication with rustic, earthy materials that feel authentic to this creative-meets-nature community. Think honed limestone, matte metals, raw-edge oak tables, soft drapery that catches every breeze.

We’ve redesigned homes near Tuna Canyon Park with full glass walls, collaborated with builders halfway up Entrada Road, and solved layout challenges for families in Topanga Oaks. No guesswork—just hyperlocal expertise.

We’re small by design. You get direct access to our senior team and a cohesive design experience from kickoff to install. Our job? Make your home’s transformation feel seamless, exciting, and something you'll actually enjoy.
