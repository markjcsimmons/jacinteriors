The Vibe

Living in Van Nuys

Van Nuys may be a lesser-known Valley gem to some, but to us at JAC Interiors, it’s a vibrant canvas of untapped architectural charm, mid-century roots, and sprawling suburban homes with so much potential. We’ve called the Los Angeles area home for over a decade, so whether you’re in a traditional ranch near Chandler Boulevard or updating a 1950s fixer on Victory, we know just how to unlock the unique possibilities of your space.

JAC Interiors is a full-service interior design studio with offices in Los Angeles and Colorado, and recent expansion into Florida’s Gold Coast. Led by Andrea Putman and Timothy Mersman—both trained at the Otis School of Arts and Sciences—our studio offers high-touch customized design that turns your interiors into lifestyle-forward sanctuaries built for entertaining, working, and seriously good living. If you're looking for a strategic, bold, and collaborative Van Nuys interior designer, you've found the right team.

Approach

Why JAC Interiors

We thrive in homes with character—whether post-war ranches off Woodman Ave or newer builds tucked near Sepulveda Basin. With Van Nuys’ mix of original architecture and increasing renovation momentum, we're perfectly positioned to transform familiar layouts with bold, West Coast–eclectic sensibility.

With long golden afternoons and wide windows capturing that iconic SoCal glow, Van Nuys light calls for the right paint sheens and materials that play naturally with warmth. We consider lighting—from scones to ceiling treatments—as a foundational design element, not an afterthought.

At JAC Interiors, we believe the best results come from partnership. We’ll shape every detail with your tastes front and center while layering in our signature style: collected, confident, cool—but never cookie-cutter.

No need to wrangle vendors or manage subs—we handle everything from initial concepts to coordinating with your GC or recommending one of ours. You get to focus on the fun stuff (tile inspiration boards, anyone?), while we do the heavy lifting.

Van Nuys homes are some of the most under-loved in LA—and we consider that an opportunity. Whether you’re carving out an artist's studio in a backyard ADU or opening up your living space with panoramic sliders, we design with both your lifestyle and resale value in mind.

Designing in Van Nuys gives us access to some of our favorite showrooms and local vendors that bring thoughtfully sourced style to your doorstep.

For high-end but approachable plumbing and hardware selections, David Home Design off Pico is always a go-to for us. Their sense of timeless style and service makes them a trusted partner when we’re designing a custom kitchen or bath.

We also frequent the FORM Design Studio in nearby Studio City—an incredible, newly curated showroom just a few freeway minutes away. With locally made furniture and sustainable materials, it reflects exactly the vibe our Valley projects deserve.

Lighting? We’re loyal to Arteriors on Melrose for their sculptural, forward-thinking fixtures (and yes, we’ve made special Saturday trips just to browse). When styling a fireplace wall or sourcing something unexpected for that Van Nuys powder room, this is our not-so-secret weapon.
