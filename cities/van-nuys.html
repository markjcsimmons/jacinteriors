WHY CHOOSE JAC INTERIORS IN VAN NUYS?

We thrive in homes with character—whether post-war ranches off Woodman Ave or newer builds tucked near Sepulveda Basin. With Van Nuys’ mix of original architecture and increasing renovation momentum, we're perfectly positioned to transform familiar layouts with bold, West Coast–eclectic sensibility.

With long golden afternoons and wide windows capturing that iconic SoCal glow, Van Nuys light calls for the right paint sheens and materials that play naturally with warmth. We consider lighting—from scones to ceiling treatments—as a foundational design element, not an afterthought.

At JAC Interiors, we believe the best results come from partnership. We’ll shape every detail with your tastes front and center while layering in our signature style: collected, confident, cool—but never cookie-cutter.

LOCAL RESOURCES & VAN NUYS DESIGN DESTINATIONS

Designing in Van Nuys gives us access to some of our favorite showrooms and local vendors that bring thoughtfully sourced style to your doorstep.

For high-end but approachable plumbing and hardware selections, David Home Design off Pico is always a go-to for us. Their sense of timeless style and service makes them a trusted partner when we’re designing a custom kitchen or bath.

We also frequent the FORM Design Studio in nearby Studio City—an incredible, newly curated showroom just a few freeway minutes away. With locally made furniture and sustainable materials, it reflects exactly the vibe our Valley projects deserve.
