Living in Bal Harbour / Bay Harbor Islands

Bal Harbour and Bay Harbor Islands—two of Miami-Dade’s most refined enclaves—offer nothing short of an idyllic coastal backdrop for inspired living. As longtime admirers of the delicate balance these communities strike between laid-back island charm and modern elegance, we at JAC Interiors couldn’t resist expanding our services to this iconic region.

Our design approach is deeply rooted in creating spaces that are not only beautiful but purpose-driven—whether we’re crafting a hurricane-ready primary suite with custom-built millwork or transforming a Mediterranean-style villa off Kane Concourse with a breezy modern interior that celebrates the South Florida light. At JAC, we approach every project like it’s our own home, with meticulous attention to craftsmanship, comfort, and creativity—especially when considering the unique climate and architectural diversity here.

Why Choose JAC Interiors for Bal Harbour / Bay Harbor Islands

From the breezy balconies of the Ritz-Carlton residences to the iconic glass façades on Collins Ave, we understand what luxury looks and feels like in this coastal corridor. We select materials that stand up to Florida’s humidity and ocean air—think marine-grade metals and high-quality composite woods with longevity in mind.

This isn’t mass-market décor. Our collaborative style ensures your space becomes a curated story. Whether you’re updating a 1950s bayfront bungalow or purchasing in the new Four Seasons Residences, we approach every design as a bespoke solution tailored to your lifestyle.

We draw from local design sensibilities and elevate them with global influences. Working in concert with artisans, showrooms, and luxury vendors from Coral Gables to Wynwood, our designs are as informed as they are inspired.

From contractor coordination to finish scheduling—we’ve got you covered. We’re known across CA, CO, and now FL for taking projects from chaotic to calm, turning high-end construction into an enjoyable creative process for our clients.

Already known for our laid-back West Coast vibe and bold mountain modern interiors, our Bal Harbour designs filter those influences through a subtropical lens—creating unmatched cohesion in second homes or seasonal retreats.
