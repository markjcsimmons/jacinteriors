A Space as Tailored as the Address

Bal Harbour and Bay Harbor Islands—two of Miami-Dade’s most refined enclaves—offer nothing short of an idyllic coastal backdrop for inspired living. As longtime admirers of the delicate balance these communities strike between laid-back island charm and modern elegance, we at JAC Interiors couldn’t resist expanding our services to this iconic region.

Our design approach is deeply rooted in creating spaces that are not only beautiful but purpose-driven—whether we’re crafting a hurricane-ready primary suite with custom-built millwork or transforming a Mediterranean-style villa off Kane Concourse with a breezy modern interior that celebrates the South Florida light. At JAC, we approach every project like it’s our own home, with meticulous attention to craftsmanship, comfort, and creativity—especially when considering the unique climate and architectural diversity here.

Why Choose JAC Interiors in Bal Harbour & Bay Harbor Islands

We select materials that stand up to Florida’s humidity and ocean air—think marine-grade metals and high-quality composite woods with longevity in mind.
