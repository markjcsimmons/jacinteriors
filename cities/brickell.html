Why Choose JAC Interiors in Brickell & Downtown Miami?

Designing for condos in Brickell’s skyline comes with its own set of thrills and fine print. From elevator access during remodels to navigating HOA requirements, we’ve got the inside knowledge to handle it all—gracefully.

Let’s be honest: there’s nothing like Miami’s light. It shifts throughout the day, touches every surface, and we plan for it at every phase—from selecting paint finishes to orienting sofas where the sunset hits just right.

Durability matters in this climate. That’s why we selectively source performance fabrics, corrosion-resistant metals, and humidity-friendly materials that thrive in Brickell’s weather—without sacrificing style.

Local Resources & Collaborators

When designing in Downtown Miami, we lean on the best local resources to bring projects to life. We love to source curated statement pieces and soft goods at Luminaire on Biscayne Blvd—always ahead of the curve in materials and design relevance. For tile and surfacing, Design Depot in Coral Gables is a go-to, offering high-end Euro imports ideal for Brickell’s modern bones.

We also like to dig into edgy artisan work at places like CRAFT in Little River—their lighting installations and sculptural furniture accents bring a layer of storytelling that really resonates in Miami’s expressive spaces. And when we need precision in cabinetry and millwork? Schrapper’s Fine Cabinetry in Jupiter delivers exactly the craftsmanship our clients expect.
