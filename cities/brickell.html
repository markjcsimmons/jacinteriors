Living in Brickell / Downtown Miami

Brickell and Downtown Miami have always had an energy that’s equal parts international glamour and sleek cosmopolitan rhythm. Whether you're nestled in a glass-wrapped unit in Brickell Heights or restoring a 1920s Art Deco gem near the Miami River, we understand what makes design in the Magic City so unique—and so personal.

At JAC Interiors, we bring over a decade of luxury residential and commercial design experience to Florida’s Gold Coast. Guided by our “crafty hustle” philosophy and fueled by creativity, logistics, and a whole lot of collaboration, we create layered, expressive interiors that speak to your style and perform beautifully in South Florida’s distinct climate.

So whether you're tackling full-home renovations in Brickell Key or curating the perfect eclectic pied-à-terre on Biscayne Boulevard, you can count on JAC Interiors to deliver design solutions that look effortless—and feel like home.

Why Choose JAC Interiors for Brickell / Downtown Miami

Designing for condos in Brickell’s skyline comes with its own set of thrills and fine print. From elevator access during remodels to navigating HOA requirements, we’ve got the inside knowledge to handle it all—gracefully.

Let’s be honest: there’s nothing like Miami’s light. It shifts throughout the day, touches every surface, and we plan for it at every phase—from selecting paint finishes to orienting sofas where the sunset hits just right.

Durability matters in this climate. That’s why we selectively source performance fabrics, corrosion-resistant metals, and humidity-friendly materials that thrive in Brickell’s weather—without sacrificing style.

We love the creative pulse of places like Wynwood and the Design District. You’ll find inspiration from both in our interiors—from edgy contemporary art to handcrafted, locally sourced accessories.

We design like it's our own home—because we treat your space with the same respect and passion. Our clients are part of the process from mood boarding to final styling, and we keep the vibe relaxed, elevated, and always forward-thinking.
