Helping Los Angeles Rebuild

Los Angeles is still healing from recent wildfire seasons. Whether you're a home-owner starting over, a developer filling critical housing gaps, or an architect/GC managing multiple rebuilds, JAC Interiors brings clarity and design excellence to every square foot.

Our mission: help you rebuild faster, within budget, and more beautifully—without surprises.

What We Deliver on a Rebuild Project

Transparent Pricing

Per-square-foot pricing model that keeps every project predictable from day one. No hidden fees or surprises.

Smart Finish Selection

Identify finish options that balance durability, wildfire resilience, and aesthetics for long-lasting beauty.

Complete Specifications

Detailed specs for all hard-scape finishes, materials, and design elements for seamless contractor coordination.

Budget Management

Keep your rebuild on budget with clear pricing tiers and comprehensive project management.

Our Rebuild Pricing Tiers

Simple, per-square-foot pricing that covers design fees plus specification and procurement of all hard-scape finishes. Contractor labor and furnishings are separate.

Tier 1 - Essential

For rapid, budget-savvy rebuilds. Quality finishes and smart design that gets you home quickly without compromising on style. Includes complete design plans, material specifications, contractor coordination, and budget-friendly finishes.

Tier 2 - Premium (Most Popular)

For forever homes and flagship specs. Elevated finishes and thoughtful design that stands the test of time. Everything in Essential plus premium materials, custom details, and enhanced finishes.

Tier 3 - Signature

For fully bespoke, showcase spaces. Luxury finishes and custom design that creates a truly exceptional home. Everything in Premium plus luxury materials, fully custom design, and showcase finishes.
