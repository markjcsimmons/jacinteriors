About Us

Who We Are

A full-service interior design studio with the vision and organizational skills to make beautiful transformations happen.

Our Story

JAC Interiors was founded in 2012 and is owned and managed by designers Andrea Putman and Timothy Mersman.

JAC Interiors is a full-service design studio in Los Angeles and Florida that can convert your home into the place of your dreams. Our unique vision and creativity are what is needed to result in a true transformation.

What sets us apart is that we never push you into a specific look. Instead, we collaborate with you on your visions and desires, combining them to create your unique aesthetic in a functional way. Our entire team provides the best results by taking your requests and creatively finding the solutions that match for style and comfort in your home.

Our clients have witnessed comprehensive creations that have elevated their environment from the mundane to the magnificent due to our superior attention to detail.

Our Approach

What Makes Us Different

Intentional Design

We believe every element in a space should serve a purpose — whether it's to inspire, to comfort, or to function beautifully. Our designs are thoughtful, purposeful, and always rooted in your lifestyle needs.

Collaboration First

We see every project as a partnership. By listening closely, sharing ideas openly, and respecting your vision, we turn your goals into reality. Together, we co-create interiors that truly reflect who you are.

Timeless Aesthetics

We design with longevity in mind. By blending classic elements with modern sensibility, we create interiors that remain stylish, functional, and meaningful — spaces that age gracefully.

Meticulous Execution

Our comprehensive ingenuity and meticulous project management ensure that every detail is perfect. We work with trusted vendors and skilled craftsmen to bring your vision to life.

Transparent Pricing

We believe in fair, transparent, and highly competitive pricing. We obtain bids from up to three trusted vendors for every project element, ensuring you receive the best value without compromising on quality.

Reliable Timelines

We do our best to stick to project schedules and keep you informed every step of the way. All hours are logged, and we maintain clear communication if delays are expected. Your time matters to us.
