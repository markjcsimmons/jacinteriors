Brentwood Interior Design

If you know Brentwood, you know it's that perfectly balanced blend of refined and relaxed. It's Sunday strolls at the Brentwood Farmers Market, polished lunches on San Vicente, and canyon breezes that roll in with the setting sun. At JAC Interiors, we live and breathe this Westside rhythm, designing interiors that mirror Brentwood's effortless California sophistication.

As a trusted Brentwood interior designer, we've crafted homes tucked into Mandeville Canyon with natural textures and elevated neutrals, opened up dated Mediterranean estates off Tigertail, and even helped reimagine original mid-century structures near Crestwood Hills. And here's what we've learned: Whether it's designing around that perfect golden-hour light or curating finishes that stand up to your open-air indoor-outdoor lifestyle, great design in Brentwood starts with a strong point of view and deep local insight.

Our Signature Services in Brentwood

At JAC Interiors, we offer comprehensive interior design services tailored to Brentwood living, including:

  • Interior architecture and floor plan development
  • Custom luxury kitchen and bath design
  • Renovation and new construction design-build services
  • Bespoke millwork and built-ins
  • Custom furniture and cabinetry design
  • Curated art and lighting selection
  • Outdoor living and entertainment environments
  • 3D rendering and spatial planning
  • Rebuild support through JAC's wildfire recovery programs
  • Contractor collaboration and site coordination

From reimagining a Brentwood Glen bungalow with earthy calm to transforming a family estate in Brentwood Park into a curated modern retreat, our designs celebrate your lifestyle, while honoring the details that make Brentwood special.

Why Choose JAC Interiors in Brentwood

Grounded in Local Insight

From navigating Brentwood's HOA regulations to working within the architectural character of neighborhoods like Brentwood Circle and Kenter Canyon, we bring practical knowledge alongside inspired design.

Naturally Westside Aesthetic

Brentwood's signature look is where laid-back meets luxurious. We layer organic textures, sun-washed palettes, and sculptural accents that feel right at home in your canyon view or garden-set residence.

Loved by Local Builders and Homeowners

We collaborate effortlessly with local contractors and architects, streamlining every phase of your project. Our partnerships mean better execution and beautiful results.

Design That Reflects You

Your home should feel personal and lived in, never like a showroom. We layer family heirlooms with contemporary pieces for that effortlessly curated vibe.

Thoughtful Materials for Your Lifestyle

Brentwood's climate asks for smart design choices: UV-friendly window treatments, sustainable woods that withstand that low, slanting afternoon light, and textiles that look luxe but handle the occasional glass of Cabernet.

Local Resources & Collaborators

Our work is only as good as the trusted vendors and design partners we source from here in the city. When sourcing lighting for a Crestwood Hills home, we often turn to—and often get inspired at—Rejuvenation in the Brentwood Country Mart. For bespoke cabinetry or millwork, Beaubois Woodworks in Santa Monica delivers the kind of craftsmanship and clean detailing our Brentwood clients crave.

Need a killer faucet or unforgettable stone slab? We make regular stops at Ferguson on Pico and the Walker Zanger showroom in West LA. These partners don't just offer product, they offer personality, flexibility and service, which we extend to you.
