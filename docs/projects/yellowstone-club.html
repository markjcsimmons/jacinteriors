The Vision

Mountain Retreat in Montana blending refined textures with modern simplicity.

Design Approach

This design-build house is tucked right into the Rocky Mountain range at the base of Pioneer Mountain, the site of the uber exclusive, members only Yellowstone Club.

Key Features

JAC was lucky enough to work with the architect and builder to develop, design, build and furnish this family owned ski chateau.

Living Spaces

The home has 5 bedrooms with two master bedrooms both complete with fireplaces and outdoor space to take in the tree lined mountain views accenting the custom interiors.

Refined Details

The dining room and living room share a giant stone slabbed fireplace and look out onto the extra large hot tub with mountain views beyond.

The Result

There’s a kids bunk room which can sleep 8 in full sized bunk beds near the upstairs lounge and bar area. This modern smart home is a study in textures and contrasts with JAC Interiors custom made furnishing in every room and loads of wall coverings and treatments to accent the Mountain Modern style and Montana interior design we strived for. The result is mountain chic interiors and a casual and fun family home that strikes the right chord for entertaining all season round.
