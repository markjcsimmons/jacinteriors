The Vision

Bohemian Chic in Venice, CA blending refined textures with modern simplicity.

Design Approach

This custom made home sits atop a hill in Venice, CA  affording ocean views and breezes throughout.

Key Features

We worked with the developer and the homeowner to create a boho chic style modern farmhouse home that really tells the unique story of the small family living there.

Living Spaces

It is loaded with bespoke touches like the hand-selected matte fireplace slab, one-off lighting statement pieces, and wall treatments and papers that truly fill this home with textures and a sense of the organic to really bring the beachy southern California vibe to life.

Refined Details

With every inch of space accounted for, this home boasts a pool, pool house and outdoor spaces complete with a cozy firepit and outdoor kitchen that really packs a punch to knock out any entertainment experience.
