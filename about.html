Who We Are

Our Story

JAC Interiors is a full-service interior design studio based in Los Angeles, bringing together high-end aesthetics with livable comfort. Our award-winning team is driven by creativity, collaboration, and a passion for spaces that tell stories.

From concept to completion, we handle every detail with care—tailoring designs to each client's taste and lifestyle. Whether you're remodeling a home or staging for sale, JAC Interiors delivers inspired spaces.

Our Design Philosophy

  • Every home should reflect the people who live in it
  • Design should balance function with beauty
  • Bold choices lead to memorable interiors

Celebrating our 10th Anniversary, JAC continues to create brilliant designs for residential and commercial spaces in Southern California.

"We're different because we're not pushing a specific look, we're bringing to life a collaborative vision. We deliver the best results with our entire team to achieve our client's requests. Working together, we elevate your vision by collaborating with the client and each other. You'll receive intelligent, kind, and incredibly creative forces working for you!"

What Makes Us Different

Crafty Hustle

We are task-oriented and responsive. We're on it, all the time, tracking all the details. Our team is a rare combo of creative vision and detailed project management.

Transformation

From re-imagining an entire house or commercial space– adding the finishing touches with the right light switch, or choosing the perfect backsplash…we turn concepts into reality.

Enjoyment

We love what we do. We are mindful of creating a trusting, safe, comfortable environment so clients can relax, let go, and enjoy the process, confident that we've got their best interest at heart.

Meet the Team

JAC Interiors was founded in 2012 and is owned and managed by designers Andrea Putman and Timothy Mersman.

Andrea Putman

LOS ANGELES & FLORIDA

Andrea has been an interior designer for over 20 years, working in Los Angeles, Atlanta and London and has been featured on TV and in various design publications. She trained at the Otis School of Arts and Sciences and also has a Masters in psychology.

Timothy Mersman

LOS ANGELES & FLORIDA

As an interior design graduate of FIDM in Los Angeles, Timothy has been merging form and function to create beautifully designed spaces for a range of clients for over 15 years.
