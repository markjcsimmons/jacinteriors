Beverly Hills Alpine

A refined Spanish-style residence transformed into a modern family sanctuary

Client
Private Client
Location
Beverly Hills, CA
Style
Modern Spanish
Year
2023

The Vision

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills, this Spanish-style home was ripe for a remodel. We worked with the owners to create a modernized version of their dream home, with a whole new color scheme inside and out.

The Kitchen

The custom kitchen features a large center island topped with high-contrast quartz slabs that can accommodate bar seating at one end for a more modern look. A wall-to-wall dining banquette and custom table were built to fit their large family.

Heart of the Home

This kitchen is now the main gathering place, thanks to the extra seating we created with the oversized banquette versus the small dining table they originally had in that space.

Living Spaces

In the family room, we designed a custom bar cabinet and wall shelves which flanked the fireplace slab. We reconfigured the furnishings to allow for maximum entertainment appeal and elevated comfort.

Refined Details

In the dining room, we reinvented the client's heirloom chairs by recovering them in a wow-factor fabric, providing much-needed visual punch. The oversized chandelier is the statement piece that pairs well with vintage pieces.

Outdoor Sanctuary

The backyard pool was rebuilt to be smaller, allowing for various seating and gathering spaces. Varying ground materials - from pea gravel to decking to white concrete - add natural texture alongside the beautiful greenery and palm trees.
