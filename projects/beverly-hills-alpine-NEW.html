Project Story

Client Vision

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills, this Spanish-style home was ripe for a remodel. We worked with the owners to create a modernized version of their dream home, with a whole new color scheme inside and out.

The custom kitchen features a large center island topped with high-contrast quartz slabs that can accommodate bar seating at one end for a more modern look. A wall-to-wall dining banquette and custom table were built to fit their large family under the window.

This kitchen is now the heart of the home - the main gathering place due to the extra seating we created with the oversized banquette versus the small dining table they originally had.

In the family room, we designed a custom bar cabinet and wall shelves which flanked the fireplace slab we sourced to update the space. We reconfigured the furnishings to allow for maximum entertainment appeal and elevated comfort.

The home now feels like an oasis to the busy city life just outside.
