Client Vision

Our Toscana Country Club design-build project was a real joy to create and collaborate on with the LA native homeowners. This stunning location overlooking both the 17th tee and a beautiful lake in Indian Wells is the setting for a second home complete with pool, hot tub and Bocce Ball court. JAC worked with the homeowners and builders to create a design plan for all the interior materials, lighting, flooring and furnishings throughout to read comfortable but elevated chic, and we think we achieved our goal! From the hand-sourced art from a local gallery to the playful combinations of textiles and textures throughout, this vacation home is a genuine desert oasis.
