Client Vision

Of course we love the Mitchell Gold showroom on Beverly Drive –super chic and sophisticated style throughout with a great assortment of art and accessories. The team there is consistent and reliable and we are always pleased with their quality and high end look. When we are doing a remodel or design build anywhere, we rely on David Homami Meyer on Pico to get us the right look for plumbing, bath and kitchen accessories for all our designs. David is a valued collaborator for our JAC team and provides insight and customer service that are unparalleled!

Solution

Creative Direction

If you are in need of the latest in modern lighting, Arteriors Lighting on Melrose is the place to be. Actually, even if you don’t need lighting, the Arteriors showroom is so beautiful it feels like you are literally standing on the cutting edge of design! Showing the latest collections across-category, including seasonal guest designer collaborations, the Arteriors’ Los Angeles Showroom in the La Cienega Design Quarter is one of JAC’s premier resources to view and access the latest in modern and eclectic lighting design at affordable prices in the Beverly Hills area. Put it on your bucket list!

Located on Beverly Blvd (and also now a shop in Venice) is the immaculately curated showroom called Garde. This warm but minimalist store is owned by a former exec at Calvin klein and Giorgio Armani, so she has a serious eye for detail and style. Her shops feature a curated mix of pieces by makers around the world: marble objects from Belgium, glass vessels from Germany, lighting from the Czech Republic. It’s an intensive study in design that highlights each artisan in such a way that helps educate one on the intricacies of blending great design together for a cohesive whole. JAC highly recommends a visit and we regularly go for inspiration and purchases!
