Client Vision

Our journey with our client from Los Angeles to their new mountain retreat in Golden, Colorado was a fusion of California chic and mountain charm. With a keen eye for design and a deep understanding of our client's tastes, we seamlessly integrated their signature style into their new home, honoring both the existing architecture and their personal narrative. Nestled atop the breathtaking vistas of Golden, Colorado, our client's new abode provided the perfect canvas for our creative vision. Working with the rustic charm of the existing wood beams, we embarked on a transformation journey, painting the interior a crisp, modern white to evoke a sense of serenity and space.  From the whimsical playroom to the child's bedroom, we expertly blended functionality with a modern aesthetic. In the primary suite, custom drapes and a canopy bed exude elegance, while the fully remodeled primary bath boasted a custom vanity, brass fixtures, and the exquisite Ann Sacks Lilac Mosaic marble collection, elevating every moment spent in this sanctuary.  Their new home effortlessly blended the laid-back elegance of California with the rugged charm of the Colorado mountains, creating a haven that was uniquely theirs.
