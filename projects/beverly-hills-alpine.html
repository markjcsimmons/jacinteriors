Brief

Client Vision

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills, this spanish style home was ripe for a remodel. We worked with the owners to create a modernized version of their dream home,  with a whole new color scheme inside and out.  The custom kitchen has a large center island topped with high contrast quartz slabs that can also accommodate bar seating at one end of the island for a more modern look.  A wall to wall dining banquette and custom table to fit their large family was built under the window. We’re told this kitchen is now the heart of the home, the main gathering place due to the extra seating we created with the oversize banquette versus the small dining table they originally had placed in that space. In the family room, we designed a custom bar cabinet and wall shelves which flanked the fireplace slab we sourced to update the space. We reconfigured the furnishings to allow for maximum entertainment appeal and an elevated comfort. In the dining room, we reinvented the clients heirloom chairs by recovering them in a wow-factor fabric providing a much needed visual punch. We also used a fabric wall covering on the walls and highlighted the large windows with bright and airy belgium linen for a more updated look. The oversized chandelier is the statement piece that pairs well with the vintage pieces and pulls focus in a good way! The backyard pool was rebuilt to be made smaller and allow for various seating and gathering spaces. Our favorite part of the outdoor space is the use of varying ground materials - from pea gravel to decking to white colored concrete- to add to the natural texture of the beautiful greenery and palm trees. The home now feels like an oasis to the busy city life just outside.
